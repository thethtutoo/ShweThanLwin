OUR HELPING HANDS

“We Strive For The Best”

CSR Of Shwe Than Lwin Company

As a diversified business conglomerate which is operated in accordance with international norms, STL has never failed to take its role in Corporate Social Responsibility ( CSR ). Over 20 years of business operations STL has undertaken several CSR activities in the areas such as education, religion, health, and civil service.

Contributions To Religion

Shwe Than Lwin is a family-owned diversified business entity. Owners of the company are all devoted Buddhist and majority of the employees are Buddhistsm too. Daw Than Sein, who is the Patron of the Company as well as the BOD memeber, is well known for her religious attitude and unlimited generosity throughtout Myanmar. She has orchestrated countless religious donations in the name of Shwe Than Lwin Co.,Ltd. Among them, the most prominent religious donations are Prayer-hall at Kyaik Ka Lo Pagoda, DhammaRakkhita Religious Hall, and a approach ramp at Kyait Kha Me Pagoda. EDUCATION

Since BOD members of Shwe Than Lwin are highly aware that the fundamental secret to the nation’s development lies in Education, Shwe Than Lwin has always encouraged and supported the education in Myanmar. In doing so, Shwe Than Lwin has donated a school, and stationery to do its part in shaping the leaders of tomorrow.
